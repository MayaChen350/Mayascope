# Mayascope

## The Story

One time, when I was showing my poems to a friend at school, we laughed a bit about how cryptic or super quirky they could sound, with lines such as "at the non-asking of the void" or "is it stealing to steal the steal of my feelings?".

They made a joke about a "Mayascope" that gives a random line from my poems aaaaaaaand I made it a mobile app.

## Web (new!)

The mayascope works almost *magically* well on the web now! Thanks compose multiplatform and WASM.

You can access it on my webpage at `/mayascope`

## Also...

You might have realized a ton of poems are in this repository. This is probably every singles one I've ever written. Please don't be judgy if you're going to look at them. Some of them are very old, goofy, edgy even or way too dark and depressing and I'm sorry if you don't like them. Poetry is a very small hobby of mine, I usually only write occasionally when my feelings are "strong". I don't usually write a lot, plus english is not my first language (pretty apparent there).

...But if you don't *dislike them*, that's good! I'm happy of that!

And if reading them made you laugh, or smile? That's even better!

To be honest, since mid 2025 I have started to try to write better poems. I've been inspired a lot from artist like Nine Inch Nails too, but I still try to keep my "style".

If you want to discuss with me about those or poetry/song lyric writing (even though I have no experience writing lyrics) with me, don't hesitate to reach me.  
My DMs on discord are always open!

(For those who are REALLY interested in reading my poems, instead of the txt file you can check them all directly on mayachen350.github.io/poems, and can share or access them by number with page options`?num=` like `mayachen350.github.io/poems?num=10`)

## Contributing

Feel free to contribute in any way you think could be nice I don't know really.

### Poems

Please don't add poems. You can send PRs with corrections, as long as you don't change the structure or meanings, and if I don't judge that those changes shouldn't be applied. I have a kind of "policy" or personal rule with my poems which is that I'm not allowed to come back and modify freely a poem the very next day/hours after writing it. After all, if I'm not able to understood what my past me wrote, then what do I even know about what I wrote? It wouldn't be nice to try to change them. 

I could go on and on about why I do this but let's just say I wish them to stay in their "full authencity".

## Your *own* Mayascope ✨️

I said earlier to not try to add poems. But you can still fork the repository if you want! If you change the content in poems.txt you could already have your own thing.

Basically, feel free to fork the repository, take code, etc, and also ask me questions about it on Discord.

## Notes and warnings

Most of the stuff the Mayascope reveals isn't really happy and lines it gets out can be really dark. Don't start taking the bad shit it gives you as "advices" or especially not "truth". Remember that this is supposed to be recreational, and that the results are based off pure randomness (or well... the programmed randomness of your device or something)

**Please stop using the Mayascope immediately if it starts affecting you badly mentally.**

Please stay safe <3
